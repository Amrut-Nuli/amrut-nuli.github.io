Regular Class RC Aircraft — SAE Aero Design Challenge 2022
AIR 1
Oct 2021 – Sep 2022  ·  Member, AeroKLE Aero Design Team

Design a low-speed aircraft that carries maximum payload with empty weight <5 kg, subject to design and material constraints, and successfully fly within SAE-specified takeoff and landing distances.

SolidWorks ANSYS XFLR5 Topology Optimization CG Calculations Laser Cutting Sheet Metal 3D Printing
  • Structures: CG calculations and ANSYS topology optimization of fuselage assemblies and components.
  • Aerodynamics: Airfoil selection and L/D ratio optimization for low-speed flight using XFLR5.
  • CAD & Drafting: 3D wing and tail assemblies in SolidWorks with manufacturing-ready 2D drawings.
  • Manufacturing: Laser cutting, sheet metal bending, additive manufacturing (3D printing).
  • Innovation: Composite wing spar (balsa-birch sandwich) improved flexural strength by 15.4%.
  • Result: AIR 1 — highest payload of 9 kg (MTOW 14 kg), all flight requirements completed.

Multirotor UAV for Precision Cargo Delivery — SAE Aerothon 2022
Competed
Mar 2022 – Nov 2022  ·  Designer, AeroKLE Aero Design Team

Design, build, and fly a stable, efficient multirotor UAV capable of delivering cargo to a specified location using both manual and autonomous approaches — total takeoff weight <1.8 kg, T/W ratio >2.

SolidWorks ANSYS Ultimaker CURA Topology Optimization Impact Analysis
  • Design & Optimization: Engineered UAV rotor hub and components using SolidWorks and ANSYS topology optimization to maintain total weight <1.8 kg.
  • Structural Analysis: Impact analysis on landing gear using ANSYS to ensure integrity during high-stress recovery phases.
  • Materials: Developed a comprehensive Material Selection Matrix aligned with 3D printing capabilities.
  • Additive Manufacturing: Optimized slicing parameters in Ultimaker CURA for ideal strength-to-weight ratio.
